In this task, I learned the importance of professional communication in the digital world. I wrote two emails, one requesting an assignment extension from a professor and another expressing interest in a summer internship. This helped me understand how to structure emails with proper subject lines, greetings, and a polite tone.

I also created a checklist of do’s and don’ts for responsible social media usage. Poor digital communication can lead to misunderstandings or negative impressions. For example, sending informal or unclear emails to a professor may be considered disrespectful. This can be avoided by using a professional tone and clear structure. Overall, this task helped me improve my communication skills and understand the importance of maintaining professionalism online.
